Chicago Deep Dish

Deep dish pizza came to be in the frozen landscape better known for football the home of the Chicago Bears. It was invented by Ike Sewell and Ric Riccardo at the renowned Pizzeria Uno in Chicago in 1943. It has appeared in Los Angeles in several dining establishments. But we believe one of its best interpretations resides in Echo Park, California.

We are proud Angelenos who often embrace pizza styles from other states. In Los Angeles we have Masa of Echo Park as our premiere example of Chicago pizza. It's your typical deep dish encarnation of what Chicaogans have been enjoying for decades. Tha Bears!
